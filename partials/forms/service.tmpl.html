Centerville High School PTO Student Service Challenge Scholarship Application

The PTO Student Service Challenge Scholarship is intended to reward and encourage activities that have a significant impact in meeting the needs of the Local Centerville/Dayton area community. High school seniors who have contributed 150 hours of community service within their junior and senior years in high school are eligible.

Representatives of the PTO Board will review the information contained herein. An interview may be required after the initial screening of applications. The information obtained will be held in strict confidence.
